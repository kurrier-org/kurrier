CalDAV is an **open standard** supported by many devices and apps.
Using Kurrier with CalDAV means:

- You keep full control of your data
- Your calendars sync without third-party servers
- You can migrate easily from other providers
- Your events stay portable across devices
- No vendor lock-in â€” ever

Kurrier simply acts as your **private, self-hosted calendar engine**.
